Food from E-Bay: Questionable Groceries, Intentional Layout

Food from E-Bay is a small photo series built around the idea of ordering sketchy groceries off the internet and treating them like real products. This site uses Flexbox and Grid to turn those odd packages into a clean, organized layout that feels more polished than the food itself.

Flexbox: Arranging the Strange

On the Flexbox page, the Food from E-Bay packages are lined up like a real product row. Flexbox keeps the image and text together, centers everything, and makes sure the layout stays readable even when the content gets weird.

CSS Grid: Gallery of Shipped Snacks

The Grid page turns the different Food from E-Bay boxes into a small gallery. Each cell pairs a quote with an image, showing how CSS Grid can balance text and visuals in a simple, repeatable structure.
