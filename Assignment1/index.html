The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F***

A Book by Mark Manson

What's it about?

Learning the subtleties of when you should give a f*** and when you really really shouldn't. Not only that but also about knowing how to know whats worth giving a f*** about.

Why did I read it?

Truth be told I found the title to be pretty funny and it drew me in. I didn't realize that I was in for such an interesting ride but it's really helped me open my eyes about a few different topics in life

Do I recommend it?

Hell yeah I do! It's a fantastic read. I've never been big on lifestyle books in my past but this read a bit differently in my mind. I highly recommend.
